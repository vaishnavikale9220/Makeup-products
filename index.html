•LAKME•


Lakmé is India's leading homegrown cosmetics brand, launched in 1952 at Prime Minister Nehru's request to curb foreign exchange spending on beauty products, initially under Tata, and now a subsidiary of Hindustan Unilever (HUL). It offers makeup, skincare, and beauty services via Lakmé Salons, known for innovation, wide reach, and sponsoring Lakmé Fashion Week. The brand aims to empower Indian women with accessible, world-class beauty solutions, from runway trends to everyday needs.

•Nykaa •


Nykaa is India's leading omnichannel beauty & wellness retailer, founded by Falguni Nayar in 2012, offering makeup, skincare, haircare, wellness products, and fashion through its e-commerce platform (website/app) and physical stores, known for its wide range of domestic & international brands, expert content, and significant growth, becoming India's first woman-led unicorn

•Dot and key•


Dot & Key is a prominent Indian skincare brand known for blending fruit-powered natural ingredients with high-performance scientific actives to create effective, problem-solving products. It was founded in 2018 and has gained significant popularity in the direct-to-consumer (D2C) market. Brand Philosophy & Key Features Dot & Key's philosophy is to provide "skincare solutions" by identifying "missing dots" in existing routines and offering "keys" to unlock healthy skin.
